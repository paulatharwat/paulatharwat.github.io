Ronaldinho

Ronaldo de Assis Moreira (born 21 March 1980) Place of birth Porto Alegre, Brazil Height 1.81 m (5 ft 11 in)[1] Playing position Attacking midfielder / Forward

He won the FIFA World Player of the Year award in 2004 and 2005. Renowned for his technique, tricks, dribbling, overhead kicks, no-look passes and free kicks, Ronaldinho is regarded to be one of the best players of his generation
